UCD Krakens — Who We Are

The UCD Krakens are an underwater hockey team that are part of the UCD Sub-Aqua Club. We train and compete at national and international level, and welcome players of all abilities and backgrounds. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned athlete, there's a place for you in the pod.

What is Underwater Hockey?

A fast-paced, breath-holding team sport played at the bottom of a swimming pool — combining speed, skill, strategy, and stamina in a completely unique way.

The Sport

Why Play Underwater Hockey?

Underwater hockey is one of the most unique and exhilarating sports you can try. Every game is a full-body workout that pushes your fitness, your lungs, and your mind — all at the same time.

  • Full-body workout — swimming, diving, sprinting
  • Builds breath control and mental toughness
  • Strategic team sport with fast-paced gameplay
  • Inclusive — open to all genders and abilities
  • Compete nationally and internationally with UCD
