Get Involved

Join the Krakens

No experience needed. Just a love of the water and a willingness to dive in.

The UCD Krakens welcome players of all abilities — from total beginners to competitive swimmers. If you can hold your breath and you love a challenge, you'll fit right in.

Why Join Us?

Beginners Welcome

No experience necessary. We'll teach you everything from breath training to stick technique. All you need is confidence in the water.

Compete at All Levels

Train hard and represent UCD at national and international competitions. We take the sport seriously while keeping training fun and inclusive.

Great Community

The Krakens are more than a sports club — we're a tight-knit group of ocean-lovers, athletes, and students who love what we do.

What to Bring

Swimwear

A well-fitting swim suit. A rash guard can also be worn on top for extra protection. Oh, and don't forget a towel!

Mask & Snorkel

If you have your own mask and snorkel, bring them along. If not, let us know in advance and we'll do our best to sort you out for your first session.

Fins

Long-blade fins are ideal but not required for a first session. The club has some loaners — just mention it when you get in touch.

Good Attitude

Seriously, that's the main thing. Underwater hockey has a steep but fun learning curve. Come ready to laugh, work hard, and get wet.
